Lemon poha

Description

Poha is a light and healthy Indian breakfast made from flattened rice.
It is quick to prepare and very good for morning time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 small chilli
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 8- 10 curry leaves
  • salt(as per taste)
  • ½ teaspoon turmeriic
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 teaspoon oil

Steps

  1. Wash poha and keep it aside
  2. Heat oil in a pan
  3. Add mustard seeds and curry leaves
  4. Add onion and green chilli
  5. Add turmeric and salt
  6. Add poha and mix well
  7. Switch off gas and add lemon juice
  8. Serve hot
